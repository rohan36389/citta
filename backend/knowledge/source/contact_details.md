Glossary

Agentic AI: Cognitive architectures that reason, plan, and execute workflows with minimal oversight; the foundation of CittaAI's "agentic-first architecture."

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation): AI trained on documents, SOPs, policies, and databases to deliver secure, permission-based, factual responses - described on the site as "RAG-Based Intelligence."

AI Centre of Excellence (CoE): A model/service through which CittaAI establishes architectural standards and helps enterprises with AI readiness, roadmap, governance, and responsible AI setup.

Autonomous Agents: Deployment-ready AI agents that operate customer support, sales, and internal operations without requiring constant human intervention (e.g., agents that "hunt, nurture, and close leads without intervention").

Universal Support Swarms: Multi-agent systems that solve complex customer issues collaboratively.

Strategic Decision Engines: Agents that analyze business data and suggest or execute strategic moves.

Industry Operating System (OS): CittaAI's term for its vertical, purpose-built cognitive architecture platforms (e.g., E-Commerce OS, Real Estate OS, Pharma OS, Smart Cities OS) that unify data and workflows for a specific industry.

Vibe Coding Platform: An AI-native, prompt-driven, no/low-code platform (used in MarkTech Products / E-Commerce OS) for generating stores, UI, content, and workflows from natural language.

CPV (Continuous Process Verification): A Pharma OS tool that supports batch release readiness through ongoing process verification and trend monitoring.

APQR (Annual Product Quality Review): A Pharma OS tool that automates APQR reporting through data aggregation and standardized outputs.

CAPA: Referenced in Pharma OS's Quality Dashboards as a category of tracked quality data ("deviations, CAPA, and trends") monitored for audit-ready visibility.

DPR (Detailed Project Report): Referenced in the "AI-Powered DPR Preparation Solution," an award-winning CittaAI digital solution recognized at the AP MSME Digital Empowerment Challenge 2025.
